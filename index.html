Banana Bread

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Are your bananas brown, spotty, past their prime, and on the verge of being rotten? Time to make banana bread! This recipe does not need a fancy mixer. Everything can be prepped in one bowl. A fork to whisk the eggs and a spatula to mix the batter is all you need. Wikipage

Ingredients

  • 2-3 very ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/3 c melted butter
  • 1 ts baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 c all-purpose flour

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F, and butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour the batter into yoru prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350°F, or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven and cool completely on a rack. Remove the banana bread from the pan. Slice and serve.
